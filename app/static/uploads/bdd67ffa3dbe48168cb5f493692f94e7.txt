**AI-Powered Career Guidance and Learning Assistant**

---

**Abstract:**
The AI-powered Career Guidance and Learning Assistant is designed to help students explore career options after completing their degree. The chatbot-based system provides personalized career suggestions, roadmaps for government exams, topic-based searches, quizzes, and a book-shopping assistant. This platform ensures students make informed decisions and have access to the best learning resources.

---

**Idea/Approach:**
- **Career Guidance Chatbot:** Students chat with the AI to get career suggestions based on their degree.
- **Roadmap Generation:** AI suggests paths for exams like Group 1 (IAS, IPS, etc.) and displays structured preparation plans.
- **Smart Search Engine:** Finds relevant websites and study materials for selected topics and provides URLs.
- **Quiz & Learning System:** Generates quizzes, analyzes mistakes, and offers feedback.
- **Shopping Assistant:** Helps students find books by showing store names, locations, and availability.

---

**Flowchart:**
1. Student logs in and chats with AI
2. AI provides career options based on qualifications
3. Student selects an exam/career path
4. AI generates a roadmap
5. AI searches for relevant study materials
6. Student requests quizzes to test knowledge
7. AI provides feedback and improvement suggestions
8. Student searches for books; AI suggests purchase locations

---

**Tech Stack & Dependencies:**
- **Backend:** Python (Django/FastAPI), Node.js
- **Frontend:** React.js, Next.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL, Firebase
- **AI/ML Models:** OpenAI APIs, TensorFlow, PyTorch
- **Web Scraping:** BeautifulSoup, Scrapy for website searching
- **E-commerce Integration:** APIs for book store data retrieval
- **Cloud Services:** AWS/GCP/Azure

---

**Methodologies/Algorithms Focused:**
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Understands student queries and provides career guidance.
- **Machine Learning Recommendation System:** Suggests careers and roadmaps based on user data.
- **Web Scraping & Aggregation:** Retrieves relevant study materials and website links.
- **Automated Quiz Generation:** Creates topic-based quizzes and analyzes mistakes.

---

**Analysis of the Project:**
- Data-driven insights into student career preferences.
- Comparison of AI recommendations vs. traditional career counseling.
- Quiz performance tracking and learning curve improvement.
